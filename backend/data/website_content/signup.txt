Welcome to CampusCrew! Create your free account and start exploring amazing campus events. Registration is quick, easy, and completely free.

How to Sign Up:
1. Enter your full name
2. Provide your email address (use your campus email)
3. Create a strong password
4. Confirm your password
5. Accept terms and conditions
6. Click "Sign Up" button
7. Check your email for verification link
8. Click verification link
9. Your account is active!

Registration Requirements:
- Valid email address (preferably campus email)
- Strong password (minimum 8 characters)
- Full name
- Must be 16 years or older
- Agree to terms of service and privacy policy

Password Requirements:
Your password must have:
- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

Trouble Signing Up?

**"Email Already Registered"**
- You may already have an account
- Try logging in instead
- Use password reset if forgotten

**"Invalid Email Format"**
- Check email spelling
- Remove extra spaces
- Use valid email format (user@domain.com)

**"Weak Password"**
- Follow password requirements
- Use mix of characters
- Make it at least 8 characters

**"Network Error"**
- Check internet connection
- Try again after a moment
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
